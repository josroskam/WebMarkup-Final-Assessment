Upcoming destinations

Destination 1 - Talinn, Estonia

Talinn is the capital city from Estonia. You must go to Tallin’s Old Town. It is an old town in Talinn with a very good history. There are enough cafe’s and pubs where you could have a drink if you are thirsty. Tallin is known of their small cars, but that’s of course for the small people. Have you ever seen a small person in a big car? Not in Talinn!

Destination 2 - Sète, France

The second destination is a destination have to visit to be honest, the destination is Sete, in France. France, the country known of their baguettes, croissants, Jeu de Boules, Tour de France, French Cheese, etc... The game Jeu de Boules is invented in Sete, that's why it is such a nice place! Sete is a city in the east of France and it is a must visit place. Put in on your bucketlist.

Destination 3 - Ljubljana, Slovenia

Destination number three is Ljubljana, Slovenia. Ljubljana is a beautiful city in Slovenia. In Ljbuljana there is the Ljubljana Castle. Only 375 meters away from the high east of the Old Town. If you are on a trip, or you just want to go to Ljubljana. This is a must see in Ljubljana. The highest hill in the city is Mount Saint Mary, 664 metres above Sea Level.

Destination 4 - Antingua, Guatemala

Guatemala, who does not want to go there, I do not know. Destination number 4 is about Antingua. Antingua is a city in Guatemala. In Antingua the sun is always shining, so you do not have to worry about that, but do not forget your sun prodection. You will need it when you are in Guatemala. The place is a must visit place.

Destination 5 - Thimpu, Bhutan

Bhutan is a country in the south of Asia, with a beautiful and especially green nature. You do not want to miss this. You can go to the Tiger's Nest Monastery. You can score a bullseye. You can have a beer. Beer in Bhutan is very expensive, but beer is beer. You can go swimming. You can go cycling. You can go running. You can sing a sing on the top of the mountain. You can climb in the tree's. but watch out for other people who are climbing in the trees.

Destination 6 - Milos, Greece

The fourth and final destination is about Milos in Greece. Milos is a city in Greece. This destination is not for people who do not like the sun. You have to see the sun as your friend. If you are in Milos and you are not friends with the sun, you will get a very hard time in Milos. Just like Guatemala you have to have sun protection with you. Otherwise your skin will hurt.
