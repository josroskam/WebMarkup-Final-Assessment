Destination 1: Krakow, Poland

What to do in Krakow?

Krakow, the largest city in Poland, after Warsaw. This city has a big history about the Jews. There are many museums in Kwakow, which you can visit any day. Destination 2: Brno, Czech Replubic

What to do in Brno?

Brno, the second largest city in the Czech Replubic. There are many popular places you must visit when you are in Brno. A few examples: The Old Town Hall, Gregor Mendel’s Abbey and Museum, Spilbek Castle and maybe the most popular sight, the Cathedral of Saint Peter and Paul. What to do in Bologne?
